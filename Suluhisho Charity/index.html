About Us

Suluhisho is a charity based in Australia and Kenya focused on sustainable, socially and environmentally responsible community development projects.

Our work includes a children's home for orphaned and abandoned children, agricultural programs for sustainable food and employment, and fair trade initiatives that empower local artists.

We aim to begin building a school and medical centre and invite you to get involved through our volunteer program.
